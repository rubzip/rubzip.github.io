AI-powered resume builder for developers who want professional typesetting and AI-driven tailoring without friction. Maintains a single Master CV in YAML and generates tailored resumes for any job application using AI.

Key features:
- **Local-First:** Data never leaves your machine, stored as YAML files
- **AI Tailoring:** Paste job descriptions and the AI rewrites your CV to match requirements
- **LaTeX Typesetting:** Professional, ATS-friendly PDF output

Tech stack: Vite + React, FastAPI (Python 3.12), LaTeX

Repository: [https://github.com/rubzip/cv-maker-ai](https://github.com/rubzip/cv-maker-ai)
